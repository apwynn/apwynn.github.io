Cocoa Wonderland, 6.8% Chocolate Porter

NOW AVAILABLE IN CANS

The winner of a large number of accolades in the UK and internationally, Cocoa Wonderland is an absolute go-to for many Thornbridge drinkers.

It has been created as a porter first, balanced and sturdy with a light roastiness and distinct touches of mocha and raisin. The foundation allows the intense and luxurious cocoa flavours to truly stand out, without overpowering the beer itself. The decadent chocolate noted are present from the first scent to the last drop.

ABV 6.8% / 330ml bottle

HOPS  Magnum
